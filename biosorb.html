BioSorb — Project Details

Materials: banana peels, rice husk, coconut shell, low-temp activated carbonization, acid/base activation experiments.

Applications: dye adsorption, heavy metal removal (Pb, Cd), turbidity reduction, VOC capture in air filters.

Current status: prototype stage, lab tests show promising adsorption capacity; working on scale-up & longevity tests.

See lab notes Contribute sample

How to test

  1. Prepare biosorbent sample (carbonize and wash)
  2. Run batch adsorption with known concentration
  3. Measure absorbance / metal content pre & post
  4. Calculate removal % and adsorption isotherms
