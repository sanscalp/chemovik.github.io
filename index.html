Converting Waste into Breathable Futures

Welcome to Chemovik, an initiative focused on developing sustainable biosorbents from industrial and agricultural waste. Our mission: clean air, clean water, cleaner future.

About Chemovik

Chemovik is a student-led science initiative exploring sustainable chemistry-based solutions for real-world problems. Founded with the goal of innovation from waste, it focuses on using biosorption as a purification method. We believe in accessible science, youth-driven ideas, and solving tomorrow’s problems with today’s waste.

What is BioSorb?

BioSorb is a project that turns agricultural and industrial waste into powerful biosorbents — natural materials that can clean water and air by absorbing harmful particles. It's eco-friendly, low-cost, and scalable. We’re currently prototyping small-scale models to demonstrate their efficiency in real environments.
