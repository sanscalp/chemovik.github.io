Cool Chemistry Facts

Water Expands on Freezing

Unlike most substances...

Water expands when it freezes due to hydrogen bonding creating a lattice structure, making ice less dense than water.

Bananas Are Radioactive

Did you know bananas...

Bananas contain potassium-40, a naturally radioactive isotope. But don't worry, the radiation dose is extremely low!

Oxygen is Magnetic

Liquid oxygen is...

Liquid oxygen is attracted to magnets due to its paramagnetic properties caused by unpaired electrons.
